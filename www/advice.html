Advice for working abroad

There are 15 million children plus 15 million adults in slavery all over the world. This information is here to help you recognize when someone is enticing you to join them.

Never give up your passport to anyone. If anyone takes it off you and will not return it then you are at risk of being held against your will by that person. You must have a genuine passport to enter a foreign country. Anyone who tells you otherwise is lying.

You must have either a genuine passport or work permit for the country you are going to work in. If you have this then you are probably OK.
